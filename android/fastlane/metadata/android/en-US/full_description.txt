iRomashka is a Russian open-source messenger built on privacy and security principles.

🔒 End-to-End Encryption (E2E)
All messages are encrypted on your device using P-256 ECDH + AES-256-GCM. The server has no access to message content.

📱 Multi-device Support
Works simultaneously on multiple devices. Chat history is synchronized in encrypted form.

👥 Group Chats
Group conversations with full end-to-end encryption for every participant.

🔑 Recovery Phrase
A 12-word BIP39 recovery phrase lets you restore access to your chat history even if you lose your device.

📖 Contact Sync
Automatically find contacts from your phone book — without sending actual numbers to the server (HMAC hashing).

🛡 Private Key Stays with You
Your private key is encrypted on-device and never leaves it.

⚡ Fast Unlock
Unlock via biometrics or PIN without re-entering your password.

📦 Open Source
The app code is available for audit on GitHub.

Requirements: Android 8.0 and above.
Server is located in Russia. Data is processed in compliance with Federal Law No. 152-FZ.
